Aglio Olio

Description

Aglio Olio is a well known italian dish that's very easy to make. the main ingredients are garlic and peppers

Ingredients

  • Pasta 100gr
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 2 jalapeno peppers
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Parsley

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Cut the garlic and peppers into thin pieces. Also the parsley
  3. Put the garlic and peppers in olive oil in a pan on low heat
  4. Wait until you smell the garlic and peppers
  5. Put the pasta in the pan with the olive Oil
  6. Add olive oil and salt until it tastes good
  7. Add parsley
