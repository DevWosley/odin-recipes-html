Steak!

Description

Everybody knows steak. The perfect meat that comes from cow. in this recipe i will teach you how to sear it to perfection with just a few ingredients

You will need a basic kitchen and appliences for this

Ingredients

  • Steak 300gr
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary

Instructions

  1. Let the steak out of the fridge for 30 min
  2. Put a little bit of butter in the pan on high heat
  3. Put pepper and salt on the steak
  4. Throw it in the pan once its very hot
  5. Let it sear and get crusty
  6. Put in a half bar of butter with garlic and rosemary
  7. Cook until rare/mediumrare/medium/welldone
